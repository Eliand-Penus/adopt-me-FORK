================================================================================
AI VOICE NAVIGATION SYSTEM - USER GUIDE
================================================================================

1. INTRODUCTION
---------------
Welcome to the AI Voice Navigation System for the Adopt Me pet adoption platform!
This feature allows you to browse, search, and interact with the website completely
hands-free using your voice. The assistant uses advanced speech recognition to
understand natural language commands and navigate the site intelligently.

2. SUPPORTED BROWSERS
---------------------
The voice assistant relies on the Web Speech API. For the best experience, please use:
- Google Chrome (Recommended)
- Microsoft Edge
- Safari (macOS/iOS)

Note: Firefox and some mobile browsers may have limited or no built-in support for speech recognition. If your browser does not support it, the microphone button will gracefully hide itself.

3. HOW TO ACTIVATE VOICE ASSISTANT
----------------------------------
1. Locate the floating microphone button in the bottom-right corner of the screen.
2. Click the button (or focus it and press Enter/Space).
3. If this is your first time, your browser will ask for permission to use your microphone. Click "Allow".
4. The button will begin to pulse, and an "AI Assistant: Listening..." popup will appear.
5. Speak your command clearly into your microphone.
6. The assistant will process your command, speak back to confirm, and perform the action.

4. AVAILABLE COMMANDS
---------------------
The assistant understands a variety of natural phrases. You don't have to speak like a robot! It understands variations like "Go to...", "Open...", "Show...", and "Take me to...".

5. NAVIGATION COMMANDS
----------------------
Use these commands to move around the website:
- "Go home" or "Take me home" -> Goes to the Home page
- "Open pets" or "Show pet catalog" -> Goes to the Pets Catalog
- "Open dashboard" -> Goes to your User Dashboard
- "Show favorites" -> Opens your saved Favorite pets
- "Open applications" -> Views your adoption applications
- "Open about" -> Goes to the About Us page
- "Login" or "Sign in" -> Goes to the Login page
- "Register" or "Sign up" -> Goes to the Registration page
- "Go back" or "Return" -> Returns to the previous page

6. FILTERING COMMANDS
---------------------
Use these commands to search for specific animals. If you are not on the pets page, the assistant will automatically take you there and apply the filters!
- "Search dogs" or "Show dogs"
- "Search cats" or "Show cats"
- "Show male dogs"
- "Show female dogs"
- "Show male cats"
- "Show female cats"
- "Clear filters" or "Reset filters" -> Removes all current search filters

7. SCROLLING COMMANDS
---------------------
Use these to navigate long pages without touching your mouse:
- "Scroll down" or "Go down"
- "Scroll up" or "Go up"
- "Go to top"
- "Go to bottom"

8. UTILITY & SETTINGS COMMANDS
------------------------------
- "Mute" or "Quiet" -> Stops the assistant from speaking its responses out loud.
- "Unmute" or "Speak" -> Re-enables voice feedback.
- "Hello" or "Hi" -> Greets the AI assistant.

9. TROUBLESHOOTING
------------------
- "It keeps saying 'Sorry, I didn't understand that'": Try speaking a bit clearer and ensure you are using one of the supported keywords (like "open", "show", "search").
- "The microphone icon turned red": An error occurred or microphone access was denied. Ensure your browser isn't blocking microphone permissions in the URL bar.
- "The assistant keeps repeating itself": The assistant is designed to pause listening while it speaks to prevent hearing its own voice. If issues persist, try saying "Mute".
- "It doesn't hear me": Check your system microphone settings and ensure the correct input device is selected.

10. ACCESSIBILITY NOTES
-----------------------
- The microphone button is fully accessible via keyboard (`Tab` to focus, `Enter` to activate).
- State changes (listening, processing, error) are visually distinguishable.
- Voice feedback can be completely disabled by saying "Mute" if you prefer a silent experience.
