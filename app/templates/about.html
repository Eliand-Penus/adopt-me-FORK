Our Mission

Why we created Adopt Me

We believe every pet deserves a loving and safe environment. Adopt Me was created to streamline the adoption process, making it easier for shelters and individuals to find responsible adopters for dogs and cats.

By centralizing listings, verifying users, and improving communication between adopters and pet owners, we aim to reduce the number of homeless animals while building a compassionate community of pet lovers.
