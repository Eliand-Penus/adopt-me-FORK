Our Mission

Why we built the Adopt Me platform

We believe every pet deserves a loving, healthy, and safe environment. Adopt Me was created to streamline and simplify the rehoming and adoption processes, making it easier for responsible pet parents to connect and support dogs and cats.

By centralizing pet records, verifying owner IDs, and facilitating secure direct messaging, we aim to eliminate shelter backlogs and build a global community of pet enthusiasts based on mutual trust.

How It Works

Three simple steps to adopt your companion

Browse Pets
